❀ my research interests ❀

In the broadest terms, I am interested in research involving:

  • changes in ecological community dynamics due to climate change
  • the mechanisms of plant species geographical range tolerances
  • different plant species response and vulnerabilities to climate change based on life history traits
  • plant species adaptations to climate change
  • understanding how resilience measures function in natural ecosystems for the purposes of improving restoration based novel ecosystems
